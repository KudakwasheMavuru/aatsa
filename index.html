Pioneering the Next Era of African Aviation

At AATSA International Ltd., we are dedicated to transforming Africa’s aviation industry by delivering cutting-edge air transport solutions that meet global standards. Established in Rwanda, AATSA is driven by the vision to bridge the gap between Africa’s aviation capabilities and the rest of the world. We believe that a connected and modernized aviation sector is key to unlocking the continent’s full potential.
